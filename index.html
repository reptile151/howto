How to cook a good rice dish

Two recipes included

preparations Rice Recipe one Recipe two end

The Equipment

  • One rice cooker
  • A rice spoon
  • As many bowls as there are people eating and one more
  • And Some Spoons
  • (additional stuff for recipe two)
  • one knife and cutting bord

The Rice

First: put one cups of white rice in the rice cooker for everyone that will be eating.

second: evenly spread the rice and fill with water until all the rice is covered at least one inch of water and cook for around 30 minuets.

last: when the rice is done cooking portion it into as many bowls as you need.

The First Recipe

    note the measurements are for one portion

first. Get a small bowl, mayonnaise, hotsauce, and vinegar

second. put a Tbs of each in the bowl and mix thoroughly

Third. Pore or spoon the contents of the bowl over your rice, mix it in and enjoy

the Second Recipe

    note the measurements are for one portion

First. Get a small bowl, soysauce, two stems of green onion, and one clove of garlic

second. cut the green onion into small rings and the mince the garlic

third. put three Tbs. of soysauce, the green onion and the garlic into the bowl

forth. pore or spoon the contents of the bowl over your rice, mix it in and enjoy
